Dear Parent / Guardian,

We write with warmth and anticipation to invite you to our upcoming Parent–Teacher Association (PTA) Meeting — a cherished moment in our school calendar where the two most important communities in a child's life come together face to face.

Your presence at this meeting is not a formality — it is a vital contribution to the education and well-being of your child. The conversations that happen in that hall shape policies, address concerns, and strengthen the bond between home and school.

We have prepared a rich and focused session. Refreshments will be served. The meeting is expected to close no later than 12:30 PM, in full respect of your Sunday.

"It takes a whole village to raise a child — and we are grateful to be building that village together, one conversation at a time."

— Taqwa High School

We look forward to seeing you — not just as a parent in attendance, but as a partner in purpose. Your voice matters here. Your child's school is better because you show up.

Please endeavour to arrive by 9:45 AM so we may begin promptly.
